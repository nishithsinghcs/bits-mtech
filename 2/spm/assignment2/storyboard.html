Push Notifications: This feature enables residents to receive alerts and updates directly on their mobile devices. These notifications could include updates about upcoming events, community meetings, local news, and emergency alerts.

Customization: The notification feature should allow residents to customize which notifications they receive and how frequently they receive them. This customization will help ensure that residents only receive notifications that are relevant to them, reducing the risk of notification fatigue.

Opt-In: The notification feature should be opt-in, meaning that residents will have to choose to receive notifications rather than being automatically opted in. This will help ensure that residents only receive notifications that they want to receive.
